The Premise

A mysterious disease is spreading across Brazil. While the Virus evolves and expands across diverse Brazilian biomes, a team of Scientists races against time to identify symptoms, uncover transmission routes, and contain the outbreak before it becomes a nationwide epidemic.

But there is another threat. As new evidence emerges, players are confronted with sensational headlines, viral rumors, and misleading claims. To succeed, they must separate science from misinformation using real-world critical thinking tools.

Will science keep up with the outbreak?

⭐
Why SURTO! Stands Out
🧬
Real Epidemiology, Real Science
Inspired by outbreak investigation, surveillance systems, disease ecology, and public health decision-making. Science is embedded in every mechanic.
🇧🇷
Unique Brazilian Setting
The game map is based on Brazil's 6 major biomes — Amazon, Cerrado, Atlantic Forest, Caatinga, Pampas, and Pantanal — each creating strategic differences in disease spread and control.
🧠
Critical Thinking Built Into Gameplay — The FLICC Framework
Every time a Symptom or Transmission card is revealed, a Breaking News card appears. Players must identify the misinformation technique being used to debunk it and gain an advantage.
F Fake Experts
L Logical Fallacies
I Impossible Expectations
C Cherry Picking
C Conspiracy Theories
🤝
Teamwork Under Pressure
Scientists must coordinate limited actions, share discoveries, and make collective decisions — while the Virus player grows stronger each round.
🎓
Designed for Classrooms
Includes a companion Scientific Report worksheet and lesson plan covering critical thinking, scientific reasoning, evidence evaluation, collaboration, and problem solving.
